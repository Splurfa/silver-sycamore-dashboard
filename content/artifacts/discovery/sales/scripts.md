# Sales Scripts & Phone Protocols

*Source: Client binder photos, 2026-01-05*

Standard phone scripts and sales workflows for Silver Sycamore staff.

---

## Phone Greeting Standard

**Always greet with a smile and use polite, professional language:**

> "Good Morning/Afternoon, thank you for calling Silver Sycamore. My name is _______, how may I assist you?"

---

## Taking Messages

If you are unable to answer their question, make sure you take a message and get the following information:

- First and Last Name
- A good call back number (and email if possible)
- Detailed description of their question(s) or need(s)
- Event Date - if applicable
- Guest Count - if applicable

**Important:** Don't hang up without reassuring the guest that you will pass the message on to the appropriate person, and someone will be in touch with them as soon as possible.

---

## Tour Appointment Intake Script

When setting up tour appointments, collect the following details:

**Required Information:**
- First and Last Name (bride and groom)
- Date they are wanting to book
- Budget
- Guest Count
- Indoor or outdoor ceremony preference
- Call back number
- Email address

**Scheduling Notes:**
- Be sure to give enough time in between each appointment - some might take longer than others
- There is also a phone script to follow to help you know what important information you need and to help determine whether or not an appointment is needed

## Wedding Inquiry Phone Script

### Opening Questions

1. **"What date are you looking at for your wedding?"**
   - Check availability in calendar
   - If date is available, continue with questions
   - If date is not available, offer alternative dates

2. **"How many guests are you expecting?"**
   - This determines which packages to recommend
   - Under 50: Micro Wedding or Simple Elegance
   - 50-100: Simple Elegance, Food Truck Fun, or Classic
   - 100+: Dream Package or Diamond Package

3. **"What is your budget for the venue and catering?"**
   - Use this to guide package recommendations
   - Be prepared to explain what's included in each tier

4. **"Are you looking for an indoor or outdoor ceremony?"**
   - Outdoor options: Waterfall Gazebo, Vintage Gazebo, Gardens
   - Indoor option: White Wedding Chapel

### Package Presentation

**For Budget-Conscious Couples ($3,000-$5,000):**
> "Our Saloon and Town Micro Wedding Package starts at $3,200 for up to 20 guests. This includes ceremony, reception in our Saloon, dinner, and a 2-tiered bridal cake. It's perfect for intimate celebrations."

**For Mid-Range Budgets ($7,000-$10,000):**
> "Our Simple Elegance Package at $7,800 includes a 3.5-hour ceremony and reception for 50 guests, with dinner and cake included. If you're looking for something more unique, our Food Truck Fun Package at $8,100 lets you bring in your favorite food trucks."

**For Full-Service Weddings ($10,000+):**
> "Our Dream Package at $12,900 is our most popular choice. It includes a 5-hour ceremony and reception for 100 guests, full coordination, DJ, open bar, and your choice of ceremony location. For the ultimate experience, our Diamond Package at $16,500 adds a cocktail hour, yard games, and a complimentary night at our Bed & Breakfast."

### Closing the Call

5. **"Would you like to schedule a tour to see the venue?"**
   - Offer available tour times
   - Explain what they'll see on the tour

6. **"What's the best email to send you information about our packages?"**
   - Send follow-up email with package details
   - Include link to website

## Shower/Party Inquiry Script

### Opening Questions

1. **"What type of event are you planning?"**
   - Bridal shower
   - Baby shower
   - Birthday party
   - Other celebration

2. **"How many guests are you expecting?"**
   - Under 25: Tearoom option ($19.95/person)
   - 25-45: Tearoom ($19.95/person)
   - 30-50: Saloon ($300 rental + catering)

3. **"What date and time are you looking at?"**
   - Morning/Afternoon events must end before 3pm
   - Check availability

### Package Presentation

**For Small Groups (under 45):**
> "Our Tearoom is perfect for groups of 25-45 guests. At $19.95 per person, you get tea and coffee service with your choice of croissant plate or spinach quiche. We can add a decorated cake for the event."

**For Larger Groups (30-50):**
> "Our Saloon is ideal for larger groups. The rental is $300 for a morning or afternoon event, plus your choice of our Appetizer Menu at $21.95 per person or our Howdy Partner burger menu at $24.95 per person."

### Add-On Suggestions

> "Would you like to add any of our special touches? We offer:
> - Decorated cake at $4.50 per person
> - Decor package for $75 (we set up and clean up)
> - Cash bar service with a bartender for $150"

## B&B Reservation Script

### Taking Reservations

When a B&B reservation comes in, collect:

**By Phone:**
1. Have the booking form in front of you to collect all appropriate info
2. Add to Rezovations, booking.com, and Google calendar

**Required Information:**
- Guest name and contact information
- Check-in date and time
- Number of guests
- Room preference
- Dietary needs for breakfast
- Credit card information for hold

### Confirmation Process

1. After you have contacted the guest, send the confirmation email via Rezovations
2. One day before check-in, send their 2nd email with entrance code attachment
3. Include parking details and breakfast information
4. If they are in the Saloon, include how to use exterior locks

## Objection Handling

### "That's more than we budgeted"

> "I understand budget is important. Let me share some options:
> - Our Micro Wedding Package starts at $3,200 for intimate celebrations
> - We can customize packages to fit your needs
> - Payment plans are available with a deposit to hold your date"

### "We're still looking at other venues"

> "Of course! I'd love for you to come see Silver Sycamore in person. Once you see our western town and beautiful grounds, I think you'll fall in love. When can I schedule a tour for you?"

### "We need to think about it"

> "Absolutely, take your time. I'll send you our package information by email. Keep in mind that [date] is a popular date and we do book up quickly. Would you like me to hold that date for 48 hours while you decide?"

## Follow-Up Protocol

After every inquiry call:

1. **Same Day:** Send email with package information
2. **2 Days Later:** Follow-up call or email if no response
3. **1 Week Later:** Final follow-up if no booking made
4. **After Tour:** Send thank you email within 24 hours
