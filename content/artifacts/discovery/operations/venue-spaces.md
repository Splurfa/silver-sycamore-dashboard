# Venues & Spaces

*Operational reference for Silver Sycamore physical spaces and capacities.*

## Overview

Six-acre Old West themed property ("Sycamoreville") at 5111 Pine Avenue, Pasadena TX. Marketed as ~15 minutes south of downtown Houston.

## Reception Venues

1. **Indoor Reception Hall** - Elegant, climate-controlled, 50-200 guests
2. **The Street of Sycamoreville** - Western town setting, 100-200+ guests
3. **Saloon** - Intimate western atmosphere, 30-50 guests
