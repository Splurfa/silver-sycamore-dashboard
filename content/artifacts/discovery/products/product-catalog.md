## How This Works

Silver Sycamore operates as a full-service wedding and event venue with three main revenue streams:

1. **Wedding Packages** - All-inclusive ceremony + reception packages for 20-200+ guests
2. **Event Rentals** - Reception hall, saloon, tearoom rentals for parties, showers, banquets
3. **Catering Services** - On-site and off-premise catering with Pine Street Cafe

**Pricing Note:** All detailed pricing tables are maintained in `silver-sycamore-master.xlsx` with the following tabs:
- `WEDDING_PACKAGES` - Full wedding package pricing
- `EVENT_PACKAGES` - Reception hall, saloon, tearoom rentals
- `CATERING` - Menu options and per-person pricing
- `BAR` - Bar service packages
- `ADD_ONS` - Entertainment, decor, extras

## Wedding Packages

### Package Tiers Overview

Silver Sycamore offers six wedding package tiers, ranging from intimate 20-person micro-weddings to premium 100+ guest celebrations:

**Micro Wedding:**
- **Saloon & Town Merry** (20-50 guests, 2.5 hrs) - Intimate ceremony and saloon reception with essential inclusions

**Mid-Range:**
- **Simple Elegance** (50 guests, 3.5 hrs) - Ceremony, reception in town or hall, white linens, cash bar setup
- **Food Truck Fun** (100 guests, 4 hrs) - Unique option for couples bringing their own food trucks (space for 3 trucks)

**Premium:**
- **Classic Package** (100+ guests, 5 hrs) - Coordinated ceremony and reception, DJ included, couples add catering
- **Dream Package** (100+ guests, 5 hrs) - Adds 3-tier cake, open bar, town sign
- **Diamond Package** (100+ guests, 5 hrs) - Top tier with honeymoon suite, liquor setups, cocktail hour entertainment, yard games

**Reception Only:**
- **Reception Only Package** (100+ guests, 4 hrs) - For couples married elsewhere, includes DJ, open bar, cake

### What's Included Across Packages

All wedding packages include:
- **Ceremony locations:** Waterfall Gazebo, Vintage Gazebo, B&B Gardens, or White Chapel with draping
- **Prep spaces:** Bridal cottage and jail (groom prep) access
- **Day-of coordination:** All packages include event coordinator; higher tiers add consultation hours
- **Rehearsal:** 1 hour rehearsal time (except Saloon & Town Merry which offers optional rehearsal)
- **Decor:** Minimum white linens and centerpieces; upgraded to satin linens in Dream/Diamond
- **Setup/cleanup:** All packages include full setup and cleanup

### Ceremony Locations

Four ceremony location options (included in all packages):

1. **Waterfall Gazebo** - Outdoor, scenic waterfall backdrop
2. **Vintage Gazebo** - Outdoor, classic garden setting
3. **The Gardens (B&B)** - Outdoor, intimate garden ceremony
4. **White Wedding Chapel** - Indoor with beautiful draping, capacity 200+

### Reception Venues

Couples choose reception location based on package:

- **Saloon** - 30-50 capacity, intimate western atmosphere (Saloon & Town Merry only)
- **Street of Sycamoreville** - Outdoor western town setting, 100-200+ guests
- **Reception Hall** - Indoor elegant space, 50-200 guests
- **Food Truck Setup** - Western town configured for 3 food trucks (Food Truck Fun package)

### Service Charges & Booking

- **Sales Tax:** 8.25% on all wedding packages
- **Service Charge:** 18-20% depending on package (Saloon & Town Merry is 20%, others 18%)
- **Deposits:** $1,500 (Saloon & Town Merry) or $2,000 (all other packages)
- **Guest Count Deadline:** Final guaranteed count due 10 days before event
- **Final Payment:** Due 7 days before event

## Event Rentals

### Reception Hall Events

**Capacity:** 50-200 guests
**Duration:** 3 hours base rental
**Includes:** Tables, chiavari chairs, white polyester linens

Three pricing tiers based on day/time:
- **Afternoon Rental** (ends before 3pm) - Lower pricing
- **Friday/Sunday Evening** (after 3pm) - Mid-tier pricing, additional hours available
- **Saturday Evening** (after 3pm) - Premium pricing, additional hours available

Minimum 50 guests, maximum 200 guests. Deposit: $1,000.

### Saloon Events

**Capacity:** 30-50 guests
**Duration:** 3 hours base rental
**Includes:** Tables, chiavari chairs, white polyester linens

Same day/time pricing structure as Reception Hall (afternoon vs. evening, weekday vs. weekend).

Minimum 30 guests, maximum 50 guests. Deposit: $300.

### Tearoom Small Parties

**Capacity:** 25-45 guests
**Time Slots:** 3-6pm or 4-7pm
**Perfect For:** Bridal showers, baby showers, small celebrations

**Mixed Tea Bar Package:** Light menu with choice of croissant plate or spinach/bacon quiche. Includes water, tea, coffee.

Minimum 25 guests, maximum 45 guests. Deposit: $200.

### School Banquet Package

**Capacity:** 50-200 guests
**Duration:** 3-hour event (banquet + dance)
**Perfect For:** Proms, homecoming, school formals

**Includes:**
- Buffet dinner service on china, silverware, stemware
- Standard menu: Dinner salad, chicken alfredo, roasted veggies, dinner rolls, tea/water
- White polyester linens (color upgrade available)

**Optional Extras:** Street of Town rental, photo booth, yard games, DJ, decor room access, balloon garlands

**Special Pricing:** Friday pricing standard; Saturday adds $2,000 rental fee. Tax exempt form required with deposit.

Minimum 50 guests, maximum 200 guests. Deposit: $800.

### Corporate Events - Company Picnic

**Perfect For:** Corporate team building, company celebrations, large group events
**Capacity:** 50-500 guests
**Duration:** Up to 4 hours

**Rental Options:**
- Sycamoreville & Grounds rental
- Tent rental with tables, chairs, white linens (up to 100 guests)
- Old fashioned trolley service
- Festive hayride service

**Food Options:**
- **Traditional Picnic Menus:** Texas Tradition (BBQ brisket, sausage, beans, potato salad) or Party-Time Fajitas
- **Food Truck Options:** Taco truck, gyro truck, baked potatoes, BBQ sandwiches
- Mix and match food stations to fit group size and preferences

## Catering Services

### Two Catering Models

1. **On-Site Venue Catering** - Meals included with wedding/event packages
2. **Off-Premise Catering** - Pine Street Cafe catering for external events

### Menu Tiers (On-Site Venue Events)

**Appetizer-Style:**
- **Appetizer Menu** - Self-serve buffet with quiche, croissant sandwiches, fruit display (acrylic dinnerware)
- **Howdy Partner** - Casual burger buffet with hand-cut fries, condiments (acrylic dinnerware)

**Pasta-Focused:**
- **All the Possibilities** - Choice of pasta entrees (Chicken Alfredo, Pasta with Meatballs, Penne with Tomatoes/Mozzarella, Beef Stroganoff, Vegan Three Bean Pasta, or Shrimp Scampi upgrade)

**Comfort Food:**
- **Sycamore Comfort** - Chicken entree with choice of vegetable and starch sides, dinner salad
- **Texas Tradition** - BBQ brisket and sausage with sides (add chicken option available)

**Premium:**
- **Tex Mex Party Time** - Beef/chicken fajitas, Spanish rice, beans, tortillas, optional queso fountain
- **Formal Dinner** - Buffet or plated service on china/silver/glassware
- **Gold Dinner Menu** - Premium entree selections (beef, pork, seafood, chicken options)
- **Platinum Dinner Menu** - Two entree selections, plated service

### Entree Selection

**Chicken:** Pecan Crusted, Marsala, Picatta, Herbed, Alfredo, Mediterranean, Santa Fe, Tequila Lime, Cancun, Parmesan

**Beef:** Herb Roasted Eye of Round, Filet Mignon (6oz), Filet Oscar, Prime Rib (6oz), Chipotle Sirloin, Filet Cilantro, Beef Tips

**Pork:** Pepper Crusted Loin, Stuffed Pork Chop, Honey Glazed Ham, Garlic Rosemary Loin, Bourbon Roasted Loin

**Seafood:** Pan Seared Salmon, Parmesan Crusted White Fish, Baked White Fish, Shrimp Scampi, Shrimp Creole, Roasted Salmon, Blackened Tilapia, Mediterranean Tilapia, Cod Shakshuka, Chili Spiced Tilapia

### Off-Premise Catering (Pine Street Cafe)

**Breakfast Packages** (per 10 people):
- Breakfast Strata, Classic Breakfast, Breakfast Taco Bar, Breakfast Sandwich Tray, Yogurt & Fruit Parfait Bar, Quiche

**Lunch Options:**
- Box Lunch (individual), Sandwich/Salad/Soup Trio, Salad Trio Tray, Sandwich Party Tray

**Comfort Meal Entrees** (per person):
- Texas BBQ, Chicken Entrée, Meatloaf Entrée, Burger Entrée

**Delivery Terms:**
- 25-mile delivery radius
- Minimum order: $75
- Standard delivery (setup, service): 10% of subtotal
- Full service (setup, servers, cleanup, gratuity): 20% of subtotal
- Rentals available: Banquet plating, stemware, linens

### Catering Add-Ons

- Grilled asparagus upgrade
- Shrimp scampi pasta add-on
- Add chicken to Texas Tradition
- Plated service (vs. buffet)
- Queso fountain (minimum 100 guests)
- Decorated tiered cake
- Cake cutting/plating fee (if client supplies cake)
- Dessert selections (school banquets)

**Service Charges:** 18% service charge + 8.25% sales tax on all catering

## Bar Packages

### Wedding Bar Service

Four bar options for wedding packages:

1. **Cash Bar** - Bartender + officer included, guests pay $5/drink for domestic beer and house wine
2. **Open Bar (50 guests)** - Bartender + officer included, no charge for domestic beer/wine
3. **Open Bar (100 guests)** - Bartender + officer included, no charge for domestic beer/wine
4. **Liquor Setups** - Sodas/juices for guest-supplied liquor (client provides liquor)

**Important Notes:**
- Over 120 guests requires additional officer + bartender
- No shots or neat drinks served
- Liquor must be provided by client (Silver Sycamore does not sell liquor)

### Event Bar Service (Parties/Showers)

- **Afternoon Cash Bar** - 1 bartender for afternoon events
- **Evening Cash Bar** - 1 bartender + officer
- **Small Party Bartender** - Beer, wine, mimosas at $5/drink

### Specialty Bar Add-Ons

- **Mimosa Bar** - 3 juices, 3 fruits, Moscato (approximately 2 drinks per person)
- **Bucket of Beer (Jail)** - Approximately 2 beers per person
- **Champagne Toast** - Included with open bar packages

## Booking Process & Forms

### Forms by Event Type

Four main booking form types:

1. **Wedding Booking Form** - Full weddings (tracks ceremony location, reception location, guest count, vendors, payment schedule)
2. **Shower/Party Booking Form** - Showers and small parties (guest of honor, event type, cake, bar, menu)
3. **Corporate Booking Form** - Business events (company name, responsible party, event type)
4. **Generic Booking Form** - All events (basic contact, payment tracking)

### Deposit Schedule

| Event Type | Deposit Amount | When Due |
|------------|----------------|----------|
| Wedding Packages | $1,500-$2,000 | At booking |
| Reception Hall Events | $1,000 | At booking |
| Saloon Events | $300 | At booking |
| Tearoom Events | $200 | At booking |
| School Banquet | $500 | At booking |

### Payment Terms

- **Final Guest Count:** Due 10 days before event
- **Final Payment:** Due 7 days before event
- **Payment Methods:** Cash, check, money order, credit card (4% processing fee for credit cards)
- **Late Fees:** $50/day for late final payment
- **Refunds:** No refunds after deposit if cancelled within 30 days

### Standard Contract Terms

**Setup & Access:**
- 30-minute setup/cleanup window included with rentals
- Additional setup time available (max 2 hours before party, charged hourly)

**Food & Beverage Policies:**
- No outside food/beverage except desserts or wrapped candy
- No food boxed to take home
- Guest count guarantee is minimum charge
- Can add up to 5 guests day-of

**Facility Rules:**
- No decor taped, stapled, or stuck to walls/furniture
- No furniture moving
- No confetti or glitter
- No smoking (including vapes) in facilities or within 20 feet of buildings

**Cancellation:**
- Cancellation requests must be submitted via email or written letter
- No deposits or previous payments returned once cancelled
- Reschedule/postpone requests subject to fee; decision at Silver Sycamore's discretion

## Revenue Context

### Per-Guest Economics (Approximate)

Weekend wedding packages show the following per-guest base pricing patterns:

- **Saloon & Town Merry** (~$210/guest for 20 guests) - Highest per-guest, smallest package
- **Simple Elegance** (~$176/guest for 50 guests) - Mid-range per-guest
- **Dream Package** (~$129/guest for 100 guests) - Value pricing for premium features
- **Diamond Package** (~$165/guest for 100 guests) - Premium pricing with honeymoon suite
- **Reception Only** (~$114/guest for 100 guests) - Lower base (no ceremony)
