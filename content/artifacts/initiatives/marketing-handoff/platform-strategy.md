## Notes

### Daily Posting Requirements

**Restaurant**
- 1-2 posts daily (minimum 10/week, target 14/week)
- Peak engagement: 6-9pm for next-day dining decisions
- All original photo/video content creation required
- Different approaches for lunch vs. brunch crowds

**Venue**
- 1 post daily, seasonally adjusted
- Winter: Wedding planning content for summer events
- Summer: Grounds/ambiance when too hot for ceremonies
- Planning cycles run 6+ months ahead

**B&B**
- 4-5 posts weekly
- Often cross-promotes restaurant content

**TikTok**
- Sporadic posting
- Music selection critical for reach
- Mobile-only platform

### Platform Performance Insights
- Real-time posting significantly outperforms scheduled content
- Meta Business Suite used for desktop scheduling
- Platform rewards active engagement during posting sessions
- TikTok algorithm heavily favors music selection and engagement patterns
